About Us

Fueling Students & Families Since Day One

Our Story

Born near a buzzing engineering college, Café Too is more than just a café—it's a student’s second home.

At Café Too, we believe good food shouldn’t come at a high price. That’s why we serve freshly made, hygienic meals and snacks that are pocket-friendly and full of flavor. Whether you're here for a quick bite, to catch a match on TV, or just to hang out in our cozy air-conditioned space, you're always welcome. Affordable comfort, great taste, and warm vibes—that's our everyday promise.

Our Vision

We’re here to make good food accessible, cozy hangouts affordable, and moments unforgettable—for students, families, and everyone in between. Café Too is designed to feel like home, whether you're grabbing a snack, enjoying a meal, or just relaxing with friends.

  • Freshly prepared snacks and meals daily
  • Student-budget-friendly prices
  • Family-friendly ambiance with AC & TV
